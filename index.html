About the Game

In Dragon King, you embark on a quest to defeat the fearsome dragon that has taken over the town. Navigate through various locations, gather resources, and arm yourself with powerful weapons. Each decision you make could lead to victory or defeat.

Key Features

  • Dynamic combat system
  • Multiple weapons to choose from
  • Interactive story elements
  • Easter eggs for the adventurous player
